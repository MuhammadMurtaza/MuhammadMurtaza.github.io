Security, Scalability & privacy

DecentraTrain incorporates zero-knowledge proofs in the verification process. ZKPs allow nodes to prove the correctness of their training results without revealing the underlying data or model parameters, protecting sensitive information and ensuring privacy for all participants.

Our Research

Our research results demonstrate the potential of DecentraTrain in terms of scalability, security, privacy, and cost reduction, paving the way for a more democratic, collaborative, and accountable approach to AI model training with enhanced privacy, transparency, and financial incentives for users with spare computing capacity.
